ABOUT OUR WEB DESIGN & SEO
AGENCY



Performance | Excellence | Solution

We are Perfexion, Inc., a full-service, interactive agency located in the Greater Philadelphia area. President Randy Freid
founded Perfexion in 1998 with a focus on building websites and software development for area businesses. Since its
inception, Perfexion has grown to a complete digital marketing agency consulting clients across the Tri-State area in all
areas of digital marketing.


At Perfexion, Inc., we harness the power of great design, the most advanced technologies, and strategic branding
to enhance your professional presence across the web and beyond. We take an individualized approach with customized
plans centered around your specific needs and goals. We don’t believe in cookie-cutter designs - instead harnessing the
power of your passion and translating that across the web.


As a full-service digital marketing agency, we can handle everything across the web. From website development and
integrated software to search engine optimization, paid advertising, display marketing, and branding, Perfexion, Inc. is
your complete digital solutions company.

We have been honored to become a decorated agency over the years. Our accomplishments include:

2021, 2018, 2017 - Featured business in Philadelphia Business Journal’s list of Tech Employers
2020 - Recognized in Philadelphia Business Journal’s list of Interactive Marketing & Media Agencies
2019 - Honored to be among Philadelphia Business Journal’s prestigious list of Web Design & Development Firms
2018 - Featured as one of Philadelphia Business Journal’s top Advertising, Branding, Marketing, and Media Service companies
2015 - Recognized as one of the Philadelphia Business Journal’s most recognized Marketing Agencies in addition to one
of the region’s top software development companies.

Our accolades come from our passion for helping businesses have a more substantial presence across the web. Our goal
is to enable every business to have the foundation to grow without the internet’s pitfalls.

If you are ready to see the difference working with Perfexion, Inc provides, call us today at 610-783-7660 or fill out our
contact form to schedule a FREE consultation. Let us propel your business forward.
